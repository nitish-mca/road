Highly Expressed Genes in Rice

Based on the reclassified anatomy tissues of Affymetrix data, we developed this tool to quickly identify a set of genes that are highly expressed in certain anatomy tissue. The output is a list of rice Affymetrix probe sets, the corresponding gene models and the expression intensities.

After the results are generated on a new web page, please click the Affymetrix probe set ID for detailed information.
